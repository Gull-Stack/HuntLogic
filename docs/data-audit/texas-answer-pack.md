# Texas Answer Pack

Date: 2026-04-15
Agency: Texas Parks and Wildlife Department (TPWD)
Primary authority: https://tpwd.texas.gov

## Purpose
This file is an answer-ready reference for HuntLogic testing. Texas must be handled differently from Nevada and Idaho. Separate general regulations, public hunting, drawn hunts, and harvest survey material.

## Texas system summary
- Texas is not mainly a western-style draw-and-points state.
- Good answers must separate standard hunting regulations from public hunting access and drawn-hunt programs.
- TPWD publishes the Outdoor Annual, drawn hunt deadlines and statistics, public hunting rules, and harvest survey information.

## Source priority
1. Outdoor Annual hunting regulations
2. TPWD drawn-hunt pages for deadlines, categories, and stats
3. Annual Public Hunting / public hunting pages
4. TPWD harvest surveys and species management pages
5. Licensing and permit pages

## Guardrails
- Do not talk about Texas as if it runs like Nevada bonus points or Idaho controlled hunt structure.
- Do not answer public-hunt questions from the Outdoor Annual alone. Use the public hunting pages.
- Do not answer drawn-hunt odds or timing questions without the drawn-hunt pages.
- Do not overclaim statewide draw-style data that TPWD does not present in that form.
